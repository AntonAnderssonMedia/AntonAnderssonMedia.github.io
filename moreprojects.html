"Tentan" - trailer to a horror movie

Me and a group of friends decided to take on the challenge of making a scary trailer for an imaginary horror movie.

Lots of references were made to existing movies and a lot of editing and sound design was needed to give it a scary nature. Very fun times indeed!

The video was shot at a ratio of 21:9 which is very uncommon and made it extra fun to shoot.

I have always loved film-making and photographing, mainly my friends weddings... Often for free
